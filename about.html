About MphoEntle Bakery

MphoEntle Bakery was created with one purpose: to deliver fresh, affordable, and delicious baked goods to the community. We focus on hygiene, consistent quality, and friendly service.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality baked products using clean ingredients and modern baking practices, while keeping the taste authentic and home-like.

Our Values

  • Quality: We bake fresh and check every batch.
  • Integrity: Honest pricing and reliable service.
  • Community: We support local suppliers and local events.
  • Creativity: Custom designs for cakes and pastries.

What we make

  • Breads (white, brown, seed loaves)
  • Cakes (wedding, birthday, graduation)
  • Pastries (scones, doughnuts, muffins)
  • Traditional snacks (vetkoek and more)
